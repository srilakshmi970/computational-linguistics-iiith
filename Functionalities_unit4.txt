The Top-Level Functionalities in the experiment are:
1)We have to identify the tokens and types in the given corpus.
2)When correct values of token and types is entered the textbox turns green otherwise it turns red adn as message "WRONG ANSWER!!" will be displayed.
3)If both the answers are right,submit button is activated along with the RIGHT ANSWER!! message.
4)If the all the words with same root are selected then the simulation wil be completed else same error message will be generated.
